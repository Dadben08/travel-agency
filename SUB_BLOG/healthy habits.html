HOW TO KEEP YOUR HEALTHY HABITS ON A BUSINESS TRIP



As a road warrior, you know keeping healthy habits on a business trip can be challenging. Work demands and being in an unfamiliar
city can alter your wellness routine. So here are a few tips to help you maintain your health on the road


ARRIVE A DAY EARLY



Arrive at your destination a day early to give your mind and body a chance to adjust to your new surroundings before work kicks in. Boost your mood after
your travel time by walking around outside in the sunlight and fresh air. Stop in a small cafe or shop and chat with the owner about what makes
their city special. Then, enjoy a light meal and relax in your room for the rest of the evening



STICK TO YOUR SCHEDULE



It may take a bit of juggling, but do your best to keep your daily routine in place.
Retire for the night, wake up at your usual time, and schedule exercise and rest periods before and after work.



EAT HEALTHY FOODS



Healthy food restaurants are now available in most cities. First, ask the hotel concierge for some suggestions or take a few minutes and
research a list on your own. Then, book advance dinner reservations at the places that fit your dietary goals. Finally, for the nights you are too
tired to go out, call a delivery service to bring the meal to you.



PURCHASE A SUBSCRIPTION EXERCISE APP



It's easy to use your hotel room as your gym with an on-demand exercise app on your smartphone or tablet. Each has a variety of instructor-led
classes available to stream at whatever time is convenient for you. Pack a resistance band in your suitcase to stand in as weights
for your strength training workout
