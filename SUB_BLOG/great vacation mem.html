HOW TO MAKE GREAT VACATION MEMORIES



For kids, school vacations are highly anticipated events! As parents, even though we love the idea of extra time with our children, it can be challenging to find activities everyone
enjoys, especially when trying to raise creative, kind, and inquisitive children.

You’ll want to contact an experienced travel agent since they’ll have the most insight into what places will work best for your family’s needs and desires. DADBEN,
with eight years of experience helping families and individuals make their travel dreams come true, wants to share with you some tips on making great vacation memories.


GIVE YOUR BACKYARD A MAKEOVER



Arrive at your destination a day early to give your mind and body a chance to adjust to your new surroundings before work kicks in. Boost your mood after
your travel time by walking around outside in the sunlight and fresh air. Stop in a small cafe or shop and chat with the owner about what makes
their city special. Then, enjoy a light meal and relax in your room for the rest of the evening



STAYING AT HOME?



The pandemic has created an environment in which some families feel more comfortable staying put for vacation. You can make this time feel special by planning daily
vacay agendas that include things like trying a new recipe, making ice cream using an old fashioned churn mixer, or going on a nature hike and identifying flora, fauna,
and creepy-crawly creatures! Host a scavenger hunt in your home or yard (with prizes, naturally!), or have a family camp-out in the living room, complete with microwave
s’mores. You can surprise your family with your plans or involve them in coming up with ideas everyone will enjoy.
